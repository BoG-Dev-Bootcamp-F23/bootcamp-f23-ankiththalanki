What is Georgia Tech?

Creation of Georgia Tech

Georgia Tech was founded on October 14th, 1885, with 84 students in its first class. It represents the transformation of the south from an agrarian to an industrial economy.

Georgia Tech Currently

Currently GT is a Tier 1 Research Institute with a total student body of over 18,000. Georgia Tech is mainly an engineering college, and heavily focuses in STEM.

Interesting Facts

While Georgia Tech's main campus is in Atlanta, the institution has satellite campuses all over the world, in places like France, China, and Singapore.
